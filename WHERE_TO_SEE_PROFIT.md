## üìä Understanding Your Profit

### **Unrealized Profit/Loss**
- Profit/loss on stocks you **still own**
- Shown in: Home page, Portfolio Dashboard
- Changes with stock price
- Not "real" until you sell

### **Realized Profit/Loss**
- Profit/loss from stocks you **sold**
- Calculate: (Sell Price - Buy Price) √ó Quantity
- This is "real" money gained/lost
- See in: Transaction History

### **Example**:

**Scenario**:
- Bought 10 shares of AAPL at $150 = $1,500 invested
- Current price: $180
- Current value: $1,800

**Unrealized Profit**: +$300 (+20%)
- You haven't sold yet
- Profit could change

**If You Sell at $180**:
- Realized Profit: +$300
- This is locked in
- Money is yours

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Why does my profit change?**
A: Stock prices change constantly. Your unrealized profit updates with the market.

**Q: Is the profit real?**
A: Unrealized profit (stocks you own) is "paper profit". Realized profit (after selling) is real money.

**Q: Where's my total profit?**
A: Home page summary card shows total profit/loss in dollars and percentage.

**Q: How do I see profit per stock?**
A: Home page holdings section or Portfolio Dashboard table.

**Q: Can I see yesterday's profit?**
A: Not currently tracked. Only current and today's return shown.

**Q: What if I sold stocks?**
A: Check Transaction History for sell transactions. Calculate realized profit manually.
